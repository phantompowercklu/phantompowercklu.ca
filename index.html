What is Phantom Power?

Phantom Power is a community radio show hosted every Saturday at 10:00 PM EST on CKLU 96.7FM. We pride ourselves in providing some of the unplucked gems of the world of classic and modern rock. Hosted by Jeremy and Evan, cousins and students of Laurentian University.
